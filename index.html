my fav singer, Sep 2, 2017

Jieun "IU" Lee

Lee Ji-eun (Korean: 이지은; born May 16, 1993), known professionally as IU (Korean: 아이유), is a South Korean singer-songwriter and actress. She signed with Kakao M (formerly LOEN Entertainment) in 2007 as a trainee and debuted as a singer at the age of fifteen with her album, Lost and Found.

She was the younges on the Forbes list of Asia’s 2019 Heroes Of Philanthropy. She has has given a total of 900 million won ($800,000) to a variety of causes since 2018.
